About Us:

YLFM Drone Rentals is a leading online drone rental companies for customers to shoot and take videos of stunning quality with our latest drone models with excellent performance. With a foreseeable estimation on global drone using trend, we acknowledge that drone is going to create a huge market on different purpose of use, such as photographing, agriculture, geographic prospecting. We understand that buying a drone can be hard to afford. With a view to allowing more people to experience the wonder of the flying robots at a reasonable price, our company is set up to provide two services including renting drones and providing services of filming by drones by our trained drone pilots. Different packages of drone rental service are available on our website. Whether our customer is a professional or an amateur drone user, they can find the most suitable package at our company in a timely manner. We offer drone rentals and specialize in DJI rentals. We own a large fleet of drones, from recreational to professional, it fulfills every customers’ needs in capturing every unique moment in their life.
